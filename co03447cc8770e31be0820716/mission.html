The Tools of the Trade

Molding leather into works of art is a task that requires a number of tools to complete each piece. Here are just a few of the tools that you would find in a leather workerÂ´s arsenal.

    Rotary Knife

Makes it very easier to get a smooth uninterrupted cut in the material you are working with.

    Pricking Iron

Used to give you an even line of holes that will make sewing the materials together an easier, and more professional looking, task.

    Scratch Awl

Useful when you want to make marks on your leather to help you denote what future work you will do to the material.
